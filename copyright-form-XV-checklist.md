# CHECK LIST FOR FORM XV

The application should be supported by the following documents:

- (a) Applicant should send a copy of **Form XV**, **Statement of Particulars (SOP)** along with the prescribed fee as per **Schedule II**.
- (b) Original **Power of Attorney** (specific for the purpose), if the application is filed through an Attorney.
- (c) **Notarised copy of Deed**, if any (Assignment / Partnership / Dissolution, etc.).
- (d) **Affidavit** stating that *no case* is pending in any court of law relating to the copyright in question.
- (e) **Attested copy of Death Certificate**, if the original copyright holder is deceased.
- (f) **Notarised copy/copies of Extract from the Register of Copyrights (ROC)** along with the works.
- (g) **Columns 2 to 4 of the Extract from ROC cannot be changed**. Requests may be made only for changes in **Columns 8 to 13** of the ROC.
- (h) The application should be **signed by the applicant only**.
