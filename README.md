# Indian Law – Free Legal Templates Repository

##  About This Repository

This repository is a **FREE**, open-source collection of Indian legal templates** designed for law students, young advocates, legal researchers, startups, and self-represented litigants.

The goal is to **demystify Indian law** by providing well-structured, editable, and practical legal drafts based on Indian statutes, court practice, and procedural requirements.

>  *Law should be accessible, not intimidating.*

---

##  What You’ll Find Here

This repository contains *ready-to-use templates* covering major areas of Indian law, including:

###  Civil Law
- Civil Procedure Code (CPC) applications
- Plaint, Written Statement, Replication
- Interim Applications (IA)
- Injunction applications
- Execution petitions
- Affidavits & Verifications

###  Criminal Law
- Criminal Procedure Code (CrPC) applications
- FIR-related drafts
- Bail applications (Regular / Anticipatory)
- Remand applications
- Discharge applications
- Compounding & Quashing formats

###  Contract Law
- Agreements & Deeds under the Contract Act
- Service Agreements
- Consultancy Agreements
- NDAs
- MOUs
- Termination Notices

###  Intellectual Property (IP)
- Copyright registration forms & drafts
- Assignment & Licensing deeds
- IP-related affidavits
- Trademark / Copyright ancillary documents
- Patent-related basic templates

###  Family & Matrimonial Law
- Divorce petitions
- Mutual consent divorce formats
- Maintenance applications
- Alimony-related drafts
- Restitution of Conjugal Rights
- Judicial Separation
- Domestic Violence applications

###  Family Settlements & Property
- Family Settlement Deeds
- Partition deeds
- Oral partition notices
- Property-related affidavits
- Mutation & revenue applications

###  Miscellaneous Legal Drafts
- Legal Notices
- Replies to notices
- Undertakings
- Declarations
- Power of Attorney formats
