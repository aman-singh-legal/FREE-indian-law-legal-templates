## The Humble Petition of the Petitioner Above-Named  
**MOST RESPECTFULLY SHEWETH:**

1. That the petitioner is a senior Government servant working under the Central Government in the Department of Customs and Excise.

2. That in connection with his official duties, the petitioner is required to undertake various raids against criminals and anti-social elements.

3. That some known criminals of the area, namely, ……………, have hatched a conspiracy to harass and malign the petitioner in public by falsely implicating him and by lodging an FIR at the Police Station at …………….

4. That the petitioner, being a permanent Government servant, has no chance of absconding and undertakes to fully cooperate with the police in the investigation, and therefore, anticipatory bail may kindly be granted to the petitioner.

5. That if no anticipatory bail is granted, the petitioner shall suffer irreparable injury and hardship.

6. That the petitioner undertakes to abide by all the terms and conditions of the order of bail, if passed by this Hon’ble Court.
